A Naked Ultrastoner

Written by Lou Mortenson

A Big Muff Pi Variant

What is this little thing? You’re peeping an Ultrastoner guitar pedal. It’s a variant of the ubiquitous Big Muff Pi created by Juan and Cleggy, a couple of Gents from Across the Pond. They are huge fans of bmp pedals.

It’s pretty ugly, melted caps, parallel caps and serial resistors and all, but it sounds awesome! It’s the first diy pedal I’ve assembled and it fired up right off the bat. Now I need to drill and paint an enclosure. It’s a good idea to test your pedals before you box them up because problems will be easier to solve.

If you’re looking for the pcb, you can find it over at Grind Customs. There’s also a great build doc which will help you put together just about any kind of muff you want from that one board.
