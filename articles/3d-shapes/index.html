3D Shapes

Written by Lou Mortenson

A little kindergarden shape practice page

My son in kindergarden needed some help learning his 3D shapes. I thought this was a good opportunity to build a simple webpage with a little interactivity, so I modeled up the shapes in Blender and rendered them out in different colors. A short page with some visual and audio feedback and my son is practicing away.
